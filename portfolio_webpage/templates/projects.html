Prediting Diabetes
in selected population

Background

The study of the causes and effects of Metablic disease espcially diabetes, has continued to recieved sizable attentiion. The aim of this mini ML project is to build a Simple Linear Regression Model, that could predict the average number of diabetes patients in an identified population. Secondly to give a detailed visualization Dashboard that depicts the trend of the disease in the selected location. Finally, we hope this insight would encourage someone to learn how to mitigate against this deadly disease by adopting a healthy life style.
